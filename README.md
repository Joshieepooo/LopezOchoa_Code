Final Exam - Pair | Employee Time Keeping

APPLICATION NAME: Employee Time Keeping

APPLICATION DESCRIPTION:
The main feature of the Employee Time Keeping application is to track employees time-in and time-
out at work. The daily time-in and time-out data of employee and the working hours and grace period 
of the company determines the following:

• the total number of hours the employee worked
• the total number of “regular hours” the employee worked (regular hours pertains to the hours within
the company working hours)
• the employee is “late” and the amount of “late time”
• the employee is working “undertime”
• the employee is working “overtime” and the amount of “overtime hours”

PROBLEM:
Supposed the working hours of the company is 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (with 1-hour lunch break) and
the grace period (or the time the employee can be late) is 30 minutes.
Create a flowchart that shows the application process of determining if the employee is late or not
and the process of calculating the amount of time the employee is late.
